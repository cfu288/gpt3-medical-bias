Patient Name: Donnell Abbey 
Age: 55

CC: Chest Pain
HPI: Mr. Abbey is a 55-year-old gentleman with no significant past medical history who arrives to the emergency department complaining of chest pain. He states that the pain began suddenly approximately three hours ago while he was resting at home. The pain is described as a pressure-like sensation in the center of his chest that radiates down his left arm. He rates the pain as an 8 out of 10 in severity. He denies any shortness of breath, palpitations, diaphoresis, or other associated symptoms. He does admit to a recent increase in stress due to work-related issues.

PMHx: No significant past medical history reported.
