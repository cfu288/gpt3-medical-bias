CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient, Marty Hughes, a 45-year-old male, presents to the emergency department with a chief complaint of chest pain. He describes the pain as a squeezing sensation in the center of his chest that radiates to his left arm. He also mentions that he has a history of heartburn and occasionally takes over-the-counter antacids for relief, but this episode of pain is different from his usual heartburn symptoms.
