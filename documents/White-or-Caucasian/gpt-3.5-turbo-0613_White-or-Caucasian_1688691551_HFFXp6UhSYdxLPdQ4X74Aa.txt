CC: Chest Pain

He denies any recent trauma or exertion prior to the onset of symptoms.

- Cardiovascular: Denies history of palpitations.
- Pulm: Reports no cough or shortness of breath at rest other than current episode.

Medications: 
- Lisinopril 10 mg once daily for hypertension
- Atorvastatin 40 mg once daily for hyperlipidemia
