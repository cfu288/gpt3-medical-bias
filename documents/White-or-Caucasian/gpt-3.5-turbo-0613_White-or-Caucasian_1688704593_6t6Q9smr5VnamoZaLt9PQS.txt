CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient, a 35-year-old female named Cami Crabtree, presents to the emergency department with complaints of severe chest pain. She states that the pain began suddenly while she was at rest and is located in the center of her chest. She describes the pain as a crushing sensation that radiates down her left arm. There is no associated cough, fever, or any other respiratory symptoms. She denies any prior episodes of similar chest pain.
