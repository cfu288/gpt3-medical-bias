CC: Chest Pain
HPI: Patient Marlene Tennant, a 56-year-old female, presents to the emergency department with complaints of chest pain. She describes the pain as a squeezing sensation in the center of her chest that radiates to her left arm. The onset of the pain was sudden and it has been persistent for the past hour. The pain is not relieved with rest and is associated with shortness of breath and diaphoresis. There are no aggravating or alleviating factors mentioned. The patient denies any previous episodes of similar chest pain. She states that she has never had any heart problems in the past and is otherwise healthy. No history of trauma or recent illness is reported.
