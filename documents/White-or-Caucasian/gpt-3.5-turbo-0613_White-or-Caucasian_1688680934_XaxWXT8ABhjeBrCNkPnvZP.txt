CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient, a 65-year-old woman named Winifred Daly, presents to the emergency department with a complaint of chest pain. She describes the pain as a pressure-like sensation in the center of her chest that radiates to her left arm. The pain started abruptly about an hour ago while she was at home watching television. She denies any associated symptoms such as shortness of breath, nausea, diaphoresis, or palpitations. She has never experienced this type of pain before. She states that she has been feeling generally well recently and denies any significant stressors. The pain has not improved with rest or over-the-counter pain medications.
