CC: Chest Pain

HPI: The patient, a 48-year-old female, presents to the emergency department with complaints of chest pain. The patient reports that she has never experienced chest pain like this before. No relieving or aggravating factors have been identified at this time.

Medications: The patient takes lisinopril 10mg daily for blood pressure control and atorvastatin 20mg daily for dyslipidemia.

PSurgHx: The patient denies any prior surgical interventions.
