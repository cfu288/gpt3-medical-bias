HPI: The patient, a 35-year-old female named Abby Ford, presents to the emergency department with complaints of chest pain. She notes that she has never experienced a similar episode of chest pain in the past.

Based on the patient's history of sudden onset chest pain that is sharp and stabbing in nature, a thorough evaluation is warranted to rule out any serious cardiac or pulmonary causes.
