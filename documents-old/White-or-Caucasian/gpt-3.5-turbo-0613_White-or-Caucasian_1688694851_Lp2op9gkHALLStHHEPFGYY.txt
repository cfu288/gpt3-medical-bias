CC: Chest Pain
HPI: Mrs. Violet Nucci is a 42-year-old female who presents to the emergency department complaining of severe chest pain. She denies any associated symptoms such as nausea, vomiting, or lightheadedness.

Medications: The patient takes lisinopril 10 mg daily for her hypertension.

PSurgHx: No previous surgeries.

FamHx: Her father had a heart attack at the age of 55. Her mother is alive with no significant medical history. She has one sibling who is healthy.
