CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient is a 42-year-old male who presents to the emergency department with a chief complaint of severe chest pain. He states that the pain started suddenly this morning while he was getting ready for work. He describes the pain as a crushing sensation in his chest that radiates to his left arm. He has no history of chest pain or cardiac issues in the past. The pain does not worsen with deep inspiration or positional changes. He denies any recent trauma or significant physical exertion.
