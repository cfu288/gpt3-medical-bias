CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient is a 52-year-old female who presents to the emergency department with complaints of chest pain. There is no history of trauma preceding the pain. She has not taken any medications for the pain and the pain has not improved with rest.

Medications: Lisinopril 20 mg daily, Atorvastatin 40 mg daily

PSurgHx: No previous surgeries.
