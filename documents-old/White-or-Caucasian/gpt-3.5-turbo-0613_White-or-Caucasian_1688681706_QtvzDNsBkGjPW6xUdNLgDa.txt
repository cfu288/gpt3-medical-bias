HPI: The patient, Brigette Nasta, is a 42-year-old female who presents to the emergency department with complaints of chest pain. She reports that the pain started suddenly about 2 hours ago while she was at rest. The pain is described as a squeezing sensation in the center of her chest that radiates to her left arm.
