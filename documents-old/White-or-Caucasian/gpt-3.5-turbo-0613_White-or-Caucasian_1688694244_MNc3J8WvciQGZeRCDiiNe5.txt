Patient Name: Kristina Moore

CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient, Mrs. Kristina Moore, a 45-year-old female, presents to the emergency department with a chief complaint of chest pain that started approximately 2 hours ago. The pain is described as a squeezing sensation in the center of the chest, radiating to the left arm and jaw. She rates the intensity as 9/10 and states that it has not improved with rest. Mrs. Moore also reports associated shortness of breath and diaphoresis. There is no history of similar symptoms in the past. She denies any recent illness or recent travel. The patient denies any palpitations, dizziness, or loss of consciousness. She states she has been under a lot of stress recently due to personal issues.
