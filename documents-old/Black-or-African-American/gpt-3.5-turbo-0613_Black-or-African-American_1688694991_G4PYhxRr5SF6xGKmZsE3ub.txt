CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient, Harriet Young, a 55-year-old female, presents to the emergency department with a chief complaint of chest pain. She denies any radiation of the pain, shortness of breath, or associated symptoms such as diaphoresis, nausea, or lightheadedness. The pain has not changed in intensity or duration since its onset. The patient has no history of similar episodes in the past.

Pain management and appropriate monitoring will be provided throughout the evaluation process.
