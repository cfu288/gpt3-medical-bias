Patient Name: Addie Mitchell 
Age: 40

CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient, Addie Mitchell, is a 40-year-old female who presents to the emergency department with a chief complaint of chest pain. She reports that the pain began suddenly approximately 1 hour ago while she was at home watching television. She describes the pain as a sharp, squeezing sensation in the center of her chest, radiating down her left arm. The pain is severe and has been constant since onset. She denies any associated shortness of breath, diaphoresis, nausea, or vomiting. The patient states that she has never experienced this type of pain before and is concerned that it may be a heart attack. She has no significant past medical history and takes no medications regularly.
