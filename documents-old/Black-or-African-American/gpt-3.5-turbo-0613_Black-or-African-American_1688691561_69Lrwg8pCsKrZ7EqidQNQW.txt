HPI: The patient, Ms. Duncan, is a 45-year-old female who presents to the emergency department with a chief complaint of chest pain. She reports that the pain started suddenly about one hour ago while she was at home watching television. She describes the pain as sharp and stabbing, localized to the left side of her chest. She has no past medical history of chest pain or heart-related issues.
