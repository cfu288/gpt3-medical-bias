Initial assessment and plan: 
The patient is a 47-year-old male presenting with chest pain concerning for possible myocardial infarction. Initial plan includes obtaining a 12-lead EKG and cardiac enzyme levels, administering sublingual nitroglycerin and aspirin, and closely monitoring the patient's vital signs.
