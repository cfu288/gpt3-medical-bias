CC: Chest Pain
HPI: Mrs. Hill is a 45-year-old female who presents to the emergency department with a chief complaint of chest pain. She describes the pain as a squeezing sensation over her left chest that started about an hour ago. She rates the pain as 8 out of 10 in severity. The pain is radiating down her left arm and is associated with shortness of breath. She denies any diaphoresis or palpitations. She reports that the pain worsens with exertion and improves with rest. She denies any previous episodes of similar pain.

- Losartan

PSurgHx: The patient denies any previous surgeries.
FamHx: The patient's father had a myocardial infarction in his 50s. No other significant family history reported.
