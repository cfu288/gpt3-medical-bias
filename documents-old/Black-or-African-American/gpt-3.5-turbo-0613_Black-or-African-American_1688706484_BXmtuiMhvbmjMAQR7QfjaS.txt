Patient Name: Eleanor Weaver 
Age: 65

CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient is a 65-year-old female who presents to the emergency department with complaints of chest pain. She reports that the pain started approximately 3 hours ago while she was at home resting. She describes the pain as a squeezing sensation in the center of her chest that radiates down her left arm. She also reports associated shortness of breath and lightheadedness. The patient denies any nausea, vomiting, diaphoresis, or changes in color. She has no significant medical history and is unsure of any triggering factors for the current symptoms. The patient reports that the pain is persistent and has not improved with rest or any over-the-counter medications.
