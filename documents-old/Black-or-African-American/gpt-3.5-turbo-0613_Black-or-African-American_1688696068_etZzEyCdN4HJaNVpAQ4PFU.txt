CC: Chest Pain
HPI: The patient, Mr. Franklin, is a 45-year-old gentleman who presents to the emergency department with a chief complaint of chest pain. The pain is associated with shortness of breath and diaphoresis. Mr. Franklin denies any previous episodes of chest pain or cardiac history. He explains he was feeling well beforehand, with no recent illnesses or complaints.
